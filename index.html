About Us

As a Breeder, We want to have the puppy's best interest. we take care, love these puppies 200% and we make sure all of their needs are met. We make sure all our puppies are healthy and happy through vet checks and healthy food diet. We are hoping that their forever homes can give them the love, attention and care they deserve.

Why Maltese

Maltese are loving and a true companion for the right owner/s. Maltese are light shedders and are acceptable pets for most allergy sufferers. The Maltese is a suitable indoor dog that thrives even in apartments and small confines. They are loving and protective of their owners. They are very smart and trainable. In general, the Maltese is a healthy breed but be careful with their teeth they need to get brushed and taken care for.
